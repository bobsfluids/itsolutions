Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and solutions.

What types of computer problems can you fix? +

We can diagnose and repair a wide range of computer issues, including:

  • Hardware failures (motherboards, hard drives, power supplies)
  • Software problems and operating system issues
  • Virus and malware removal
  • Data recovery
  • Network connectivity problems
  • Slow computer performance
  • Screen and display problems
  • Upgrade installations (memory, storage, graphics cards)
Do you offer on-site services or do I need to bring my devices to you? +

We offer both options! We provide convenient on-site services for businesses and homes within our service area. For more complex repairs or diagnostics, we may recommend bringing your device to our shop where we have specialized equipment. We also offer remote support for many software and configuration issues, allowing us to resolve problems quickly without a visit.

What types of security camera systems do you install? +

We install a variety of security camera systems tailored to your specific needs and budget. Our offerings include:

  • IP camera systems with remote viewing capabilities
  • Wireless and wired camera installations
  • HD and 4K resolution cameras
  • Indoor and outdoor weatherproof cameras
  • Night vision and motion detection systems
  • Cloud storage and local DVR/NVR recording options
  • Multi-camera systems for complete coverage
  • Integration with existing security systems
How quickly can you respond to IT emergencies? +

Our response time depends on your service plan and the severity of the issue. Business Pro clients receive guaranteed 4-hour response times during business hours. Enterprise Solution clients have access to 24/7 emergency support. For standard service calls, we typically respond within 24-48 hours. We always prioritize critical issues that impact business operations or security.

Do you offer any warranties on your services? +

Yes, we stand behind our work with solid warranties. All repair services come with a 90-day warranty on labor. Hardware that we supply and install includes the manufacturer's warranty (typically 1-3 years depending on the product). Our installations (networks, camera systems, etc.) are warranted for 1 year against defects in workmanship. Monthly service plans include continuous support for covered services for as long as your subscription remains active.
